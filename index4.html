Goa - Sun, Sand, and Portuguese Heritage

For those seeking sun-soaked beaches and a laid-back vibe, Goa is the place to be. This coastal paradise boasts pristine beaches like Palolem and Anjuna, where travelers can unwind under swaying palms or indulge in water sports.
