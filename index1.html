Delhi - Where History Meets Modernity

The capital city, Delhi, serves as a gateway to India’s rich past and vibrant present. Here, ancient monuments like the Red Fort and Qutub Minar stand in stark contrast to the bustling markets of Chandni Chowk and the sleek architecture of Lutyens' Delhi. Don’t miss the tranquil gardens of Humayun’s Tomb or the spiritual oasis of the Lotus Temple, which exemplify the city’s harmonious blend of cultures and religions.

Places to visit in Delhi

  • Red Fort
  • Qutub Minar
  • Taj Mahal
