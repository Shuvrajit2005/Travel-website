Varanasi - India’s Spiritual Heart

Varanasi, situated on the banks of the sacred River Ganges, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a center of Hindu spirituality. Pilgrims flock to its ghats (steps leading down to the river) to perform rituals and witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony, where lamps are floated on the river as offerings to the gods. Varanasi’s narrow lanes, bustling markets, and ancient temples offer a glimpse into India’s spiritual and cultural fabric.

Places to visit in Varanasi

  • Sarnath
  • Ganges River
  • Dasaswamedh Ghat
